V zgornjem kvadratku je prikazan zadnji prepoznani tekst s table SMART kapp.

Da zaženete novo prepoznavanje teksta, napišite nekaj na tablo in se nanjo povežite z aplikacijo SMART kapp. V meniju zgoraj desno izberite možnost 'Share as image' in nato izberite aplikacijo, s katero pošiljate e-pošto. E-mail pošljite na naslov iotlj.img@gmail.com in počakajte nekaj časa, da se konča prepoznavanje teksta. Nato osvežite stran.

Če je v prepoznanem tekstu barva (v angleščini), se barva LED traku na IoT tabli nastavi na najdeno barvo. Če je v prepoznanem tekstu črka 'p' kot samostojna beseda, se sproži kamera na IoT tabli.

In the upper square, the last recognised text from SMART kapp whiteboard is displayed.

To begin a new text recognition, write something on the whiteboard and connect to the whiteboard with the application SMART kapp. In the upper right menu choose option 'Share as image' and then pick your application for sending e-mails. Send the e-mail to the address iotlj.img@gmail.com and wait a few seconds for text recognition to finish. Then refresh this page.

If recognised text contains color, the color of the LED strip on the whiteboard changes color to the recognised color. If recognised text contains word 'p', the camera on the IoT table will take a picture.
